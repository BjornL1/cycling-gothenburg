# Gothenburg cycling trips

## Introduction

This site is offering bike tours in Gothenburg city (Sweden),t he background for creating this site is to promote an environmentally friendly and easy way to explore Gothenburg city for tourists or anyone enjoying bicycling. Furthermore, the target group could be anyone newly moved to Gothenburg wishing to make new acquaintances and would suit well for team-activities.
