Fixed tours

There are two different tours that is recurrent Saturdays and Sundays between May and September. On Saturdays the Gothenburg city tour is scheduled whereas the Sunday trip is a one-way route from Gothenburg to the beautiful southern archipleggio (you are being transported back on our bus to the starting point once the final destination is reached). In each tour three different stops are scheduled and "swedish fika" will be served in the second stop.
